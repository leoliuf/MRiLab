Updates: v1.3 Released Fang Liu

We are pleased to annouce a new version of MRiLab, MRiLab v1.3, for performing fast and versatile numerical MRI simulation, with new featrues including:

  • Support multi-pool exchange tissue model
  • Provide simulation in batch mode
  • Provide spatial SAR and power evaluation
  • Polish graphical interface
  • More...

The methods for implementing updates of the numerical simulator can be found at this scientific paper:

F. Liu, J.V. Velikina, W.F. Block, R. Kijowski, A.A. Samsonov. Fast Realistic MRI Simulations Based on Generalized Multi-Pool Exchange Tissue Model. IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging. 2016. doi: 10.1109/TMI.2016.2620961
